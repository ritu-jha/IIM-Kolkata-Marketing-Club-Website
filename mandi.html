Event

Mandi is about the complete marketing and brand building experience which starts from choosing a product to marketing it, selling it, and finally generating real monetary profits.

ABOUT

If you can sell at Mandi, you can sell anywhere in the world

The Beginning

Mandi(started in 2008) is a national level event that tests the marketing, selling and strategic skills of the participants over a period of 8-10 days

Focus

It also focuses on the execution of theories learnt by over 100 students on more than a 1500 IIMC students, staff, faculty members and residents of Kolkata.

Developing Idea

It involves executing the 4Ps of marketing by developing a product / idea and selling it to the rest of the campus.

Learning Experience

The end result is an undiluted learning experience for the participants and extensive brand building opportunity for the sponsors.
